Reflections on the individual contributions and the teamwork process.

During the period under review:

As the team leader I participated meaningfully to team discussions, exhibited a helpful and supportive attitude through contributing diligently towards the team process by consulting regularly with team members and gave valuable contributions to assignments.

As shown in Figure 1, I participated in content development on recommendation, methodology/tools, risk management theories and practices. Additionally, I contributed to the adoption and modification of diagram 1 and diagram 2. The other team members played a vital role in producing the rest of the content.

I contributed to the layout of the report to ensure that the content in the report was fitting on one page as required.

In the second assignment, I contributed content on evaluating the effect of risks at each stage of the SDLC, disaster recovery solution design high-level design and how the analytical report can help the management to take certain decisions.

I interconnected effectively with team members through WhatsApp to ensure that we were all in tandem on what we should do at all times.

I prepared quality work and delivered my share of work in a timely manner which lead to early submission of the report for marking.

I contributed to selecting relevant sources and having a set of good references. My efforts contributed positively to the score. I will continue with the same spirit in future assignments.

Compromised and cooperated with everyone in the team for example, I formatted references according to the Harvard referencing standard in all the assignments and added value on contributions that were made by others where necessary.

As the team leader, I distributed work fairly and followed up to ensure that it was delivered in good time. I also delegated some of my responsibilities to ensure that team member had a chance to learn.

The team members were supportive and gave me a good working environment when acting as their group leader. It was good working within this team.

In conclusion, I consider myself to be a valuable and reliable team player who is able to delivers in a timely manner under tight deadlines.
