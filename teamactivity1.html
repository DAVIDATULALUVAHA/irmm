A. Target Website Configuration

The objectives of Practical and Team activity was:

  1. Recognize and investigate security threats and weaknesses in computer and decide suitable solutions.
  2. Design and critically assess computer software and hardware to create solutions for audited threats.
 Students were divided into groups. I was a member of super group Five. In group five we came up with a group contract which was signed by all group members. Each group was then tasked to develop a website using AWSEducate resources and share the Website address and IP address with the opposite team. While working with my team member in group 5, although another team member was tasked to develop the website, for learning purposes, on the sidelines, i personally configured a ehealth website named http://ehealth-env.eba-d6shft2m.us-east-1.elasticbeanstalk.com/ with Private IP address: 172.31.33.240 and Allocated IPv4 address: 54.243.142.38.
