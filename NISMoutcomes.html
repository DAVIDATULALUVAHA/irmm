Learning Outcomes of Information Risk Management (IRM) Module

In this module i will be able to:

  1. Comprehend of the basic philosophies of information risk management.
  2. Comprehend of the relationship between IRM and the SDLC.
  3. Comprehend of the role of risk management and residual risk in the SDLC.
  4. An appreciation for existing and upcoming challenges, limitations and opportunities.
  5. An appreciation for existing and upcoming challenges, limitations and opportunities.
  6. Have a chance to reflect on and evaluate personal development.

On conclusion of this module I will be able to:

  1. Isolate and deal with security risks as part of a software development project.
  2. Isolate and critically analyse IT system risks and problems, and find suitable methodologies, tools and techniques to solve them.
  3. Assess and adapt programs and systems to create a solution that meets requirements.
  4. Critically analyse and evaluate solutions produced.
  5. Scientifically develop and implement the skills required to be an effective member of a development team
